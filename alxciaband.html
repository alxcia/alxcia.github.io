OPENING AUDITION FOR BACKGROUND SINGERS AND DANCERS

Hey there! We are The Crayolas. We are looking for female background singers and dancers to go on the Lets Color! tour with us. The tour will last 6 months. All expenses will be paid for (includes the following: plane tickets, outfits, food, and hotels.). We need dedicated people that are willing to put in hours of work. You also will be paid for being apart of this experience! We are trying to put on the best shows for our audiences and need your help. If you 18+, love to dance or sing, and are in the Florida area.. you may be the perfect fit for this tour. auditions will be held at the Scented Markers Studio in Miami, Florida.
